Potato Dish Gallery!

Admire the beauty of dishes with potatos in them!


Mashed Potatos

Image source:"sugarspunrun.com"

Boil potatos, mash them, and mix them with butter & milk.


French Fries

Image source:"themodernproper.com"

Cut potatos into strips, then fry in oil.


Potato Wedges

Image source:"allrecipes.com"

Cut potatos into wedges, coat in a batter made of flour and spices, then fry in oil.


Potato Salad

Image source:"aspicyperspective.com"

Boil the potatos, cut them into quarters, then stir with mayo, mustard, and vinegar.


Baked Potatos

Image source:"smokinandgrillinwitab.com"

Prick the skins of the potatos, oil & season, then bake at 425 degrees for 50-60 minutes. Fill with cheese, bacon bits, etc.


Roasted Potatos

Image source:"seriouseats.com"

Cut potatos into even chunks, oil & season, spread on a baking sheet, and roast in an oven.
